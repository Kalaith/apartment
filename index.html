About This Game

You're not a property tycoon - you're the custodian of a half-dead apartment building with flickering lights, stained carpets, and stubborn tenants. Repair one thing at a time. Watch the building slowly remember what it was meant to be.

Manage tenants, handle requests, upgrade apartments, and survive 3 years of building management. Complete a building to unlock the next challenge!

Controls

  • SpaceEnd turn / Advance time
  • EscPause menu (save, quit)
  • MouseSelect apartments, click buttons

Features

- Progressive Building Unlock - Complete challenges to unlock harder properties
- Three Unique Buildings - From starter apartments to historic manor estates
- Tenant System - Diverse archetypes with unique needs and stories
- Building Upgrades - Improve hallways, install facilities, upgrade designs
